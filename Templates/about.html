About Us

Who We Are


Welcome to Algo Artists! We are a dedicated team of students from CBIT Engineering College, united by our passion for data science and technology. Our team consists of talented individuals with a strong background in machine learning, data analytics, and software development. We are committed to using our skills to tackle real-world challenges and make a positive impact in the field of agriculture.


Our Mission

Our mission is to empower farmers with innovative tools that enhance their decision-making and productivity. We believe that leveraging data-driven insights can transform agriculture, leading to sustainable practices and improved crop yields. Through our crop yield prediction model, we aim to provide accurate and actionable recommendations that help farmers optimize their crop production and achieve better results.

What We Do

Algo Artists has developed a cutting-edge crop yield prediction website and model. Our platform utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to predict the yield of various crops based on factors such as soil conditions, location, and crop type. By entering relevant data, farmers can receive personalized recommendations on the most suitable crops for their fields, helping them make informed decisions and improve their agricultural practices.
